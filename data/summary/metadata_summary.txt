####################################################
File meta-data
####################################################
File: Ngorongoro Hyena Project weather data summary
Date: 2021/09/30 - 2022/11/10


####################################################
Column description
####################################################
site_name: Location at which station is installed.
station_name: Name of solar powered logger device used to store data. Currently, each station is only found at one site but this could change in future.
date: Date of data collection in East Africa Time (UTC+3).
latitude: Latitude in decimal degrees.
longitude: Longitude in decimal degrees.
period: Time period of continous data collection. Some sites will experience breaks in data collection due to mantainance or damage.
air_temp_mean: Daily mean air temperature in centigrade.
air_temp_max: Daily maximum air temperature in centigrade.
air_temp_min: Daily minimum air temperature in centigrade.
precip_mean: Daily mean precipitation in mm/30min.
precip_max: Daily maximum precipitation in mm/30min.
precip_total: Total daily precipitation in mm.
precip_max_hourly_mean: Mean rate of precipitation (mm/hour). Maximum precipitation can be used to account for uncertainty in tipping bucket rain gauges.
precip_max_hourly_max: Maximum rate of precipitation (mm/hour). Maximum precipitation can be used to account for uncertainty in tipping bucket rain gauges.
relative_humidity_mean: Daily mean relative humidity.
relative_humidity_max: Daily maximum relative humidity.
relative_humidity_min: Daily minimum relative humidity.
atmospheric_pressure_mean: Daily mean atmospheric pressure in kPa.
atmospheric_pressure_max: Daily maximum atmospheric pressure in kPa.
atmospheric_pressure_min: Daily minimum atmospheric pressure in kPa.
battery_percent_mean: Daily mean battery power of solar powered logger (%).
battery_percent_max: Daily maximum battery power of solar powered logger (%).
battery_percent_min: Daily minimum battery power of solar powered logger (%).
