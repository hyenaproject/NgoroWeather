####################################################
File meta-data
####################################################
File: Ngorongoro Hyena Project weather data raw
Date: 2021/09/30 - 2022/11/10


####################################################
Column description
####################################################
station_name: Name of solar powered logger device used to store data. Currently, each station is only found at one site but this could change in future.
site_name: Location at which station is installed.
date_time: Date-time of data collection in East Africa Time (UTC+3).
latitude: Latitude in decimal degrees.
longitude: Longitude in decimal degrees.
period: Time period of continous data collection. Some sites will experience breaks in data collection due to mantainance or damage.
air_temp: Air temperature in centigrade.
precip: Total precipitation in mm.
precip_max_hourly: Maximum rate of precipitation (mm/hour). Maximum precipitation can be used to account for uncertainty in tipping bucket rain gauges.
relative_humidity: Relative humidity.
atmospheric_pressure: Atmospheric pressure in kPa
battery_percentage: Battery percentage of the station data logger. Data collected when station has low battery power may be unreliable.
